# Crimson Strider
These terrifying three-legged strider-like creature serves as the [Creeping Death](creeping-death)'s eyes. 

## Appearance
Three, long bone-and-sinew legs hold up a bulbuous mass of eyes surrounding a large central eye. 

## Strengths
* Great perception
* Deadly stomps

## Weaknesses
* Can't hear
* The eye is fragile
