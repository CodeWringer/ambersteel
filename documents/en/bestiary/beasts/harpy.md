# Harpy

Omnivorous

Tends to live in large flocks. 

## Appearance
A very slender humanoid bird, with sickly skin and spotty plumage. 

## Strengths
* Strong in numbers
* Dangerous claws
* Can fly

## Weaknesses
* Fragile
* Weak
* Unintelligent
