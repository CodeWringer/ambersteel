# Inker

## Appearance
Human-sized squid with eyes multiple eyes facing every direction. 

## Strengths
* Can release large clouds of ink into the water to cover its retreat or confuse prey in combat
* Ambush predator
* Agile
* Fast
* Great eye-sight

## Weaknesses
* Fragile
* Weak
* Unintelligent
